Frequently Asked Questions

How can I use GetYourResume for free?

You can use GetYourResume for free by signing up for an account. With a free account, you have access to basic resume building features and templates.

How Do I Cancel, Downgrade or Delete My Account?

To cancel, downgrade, or delete your account, please go to the account settings section in your profile and follow the instructions provided.

Can I download my resume to Word or PDF?

Yes, you can download your resume in both Word and PDF formats.

What are the features available with a free account on GetYourResume?

With a free account, you have access to basic resume building features and templates. This includes creating and editing your resume using predefined templates and sections.

Are there any limitations on the number of resumes I can create with a free account?

There may be limitations on the number of resumes you can create with a free account, but typically, you can create and edit one resume at a time.

Can I access all resume templates with a free account, or are some templates restricted?

With a free account, you can access a selection of basic resume templates. Premium templates may be restricted to paid accounts.
